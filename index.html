Did You Know?

Land surveying is one of the oldest professions in the world—evidence of land surveys has been found in ancient Egypt, dating back over 3,000 years! The Egyptians used basic geometry to re-establish farm boundaries after the Nile River's annual floods, and even the Great Pyramid of Giza owes its precise alignment to early surveying methods.

Services Offered

Construction Surveys

Construction surveying involves transferring the design data from plans onto the ground so that builders, and contractors can construct buildings, roads, bridges, pipelines, and other infrastructure accurately according to specifications.

  • Setting out: Marking the exact positions of structures, such as buildings, roads, columns, foundations, and utilities.
  • Level control: Establishing reference points (benchmarks) to ensure correct elevations and gradients.
  • Alignment checks: Verifying that structures are built in the right location, alignment, and orientation.
  • As-built surveys: Measuring and recording the completed work to confirm that construction matches the design - often required for project handover.
  • Monitoring: Measuring movement, deformation, or settlement of structures during or after construction.

Town Planning Services (SPLUMA Applications and Exemptions)

We assist clients with the full range of SPLUMA (Spatial Planning and Land Use Management Act) applications and related town planning processes required for property development and land use compliance.

Our services include the preparation and submission of SPLUMA applications, subdivision and consolidation approvals, rezoning, consent use, and SPLUMA exemptions where applicable. We liaise directly with municipalities and relevant authorities to ensure that your land development projects meet all statutory planning and land use requirements.

Whether you're a developer, architect, surveyor, or property owner, we help streamline the application process, ensuring that all supporting documentation - including survey diagrams, site development plans, and motivation reports - are accurately prepared and professionally lodged.

Our goal is to help you achieve legal land development rights efficiently and with full regulatory compliance.

Township Surveys

We provide comprehensive township survey services to support the establishment and registration of new townships in accordance with the requirements of the Surveyor-General and relevant planning legislation.

Our team works closely with town planners, developers, engineers, and municipal authorities to ensure that all cadastral boundaries, erven, public roads, and open spaces are accurately surveyed and correctly represented on the approved township layout.

Using advanced GNSS, total stations, and CAD systems, we prepare and submit General Plans and supporting documentation to the Surveyor-General's office for approval and registration.

Whether you are developing a residential, commercial, or mixed-use township, we ensure precision, compliance, and timely delivery of all survey components required for township establishment and property registration.
