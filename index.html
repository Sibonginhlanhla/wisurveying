Did You Know?

Ey! did you know that land surveying is one of the oldest professions in the world—evidence of land surveys has been found in ancient Egypt, dating back over 3,000 years! The Egyptians used basic geometry to re-establish farm boundaries after the Nile River's annual floods, and even the Great Pyramid of Giza owes its precise alignment to early surveying methods.

Services Offered

Construction Surveys

Construction surveying involves transferring the design data from plans onto the ground so that builders and contractors can construct infrastructure accurately structures, such as buildings, roads, columns, foundations, and utilities.

  • Setting out: Marking exact positions of structures
  • Level control: Establishing reference points for elevations
  • Alignment checks: Verifying correct location and orientation
  • As-built surveys: Recording completed work
  • Monitoring: Measuring movement or settlement

Topographic Surveys

We provide precise topographic surveys to capture natural and built features of your site, including elevations, contours, vegetation, and structures.

Using GNSS, total stations, drones, and LiDAR, we deliver high-resolution data and contour maps.

Deliverables in CAD, GIS, and PDF formats.

Town Planning Services
