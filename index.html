About

Services Offered

Service 1 of 6

Construction Surveys

Construction surveying involves transferring design data from plans onto the ground for accurate infrastructure construction.

  • Setting out: Marking exact positions of structures
  • Level control: Establishing reference points
  • Alignment checks: Verifying correct locations
  • As-built surveys: Recording completed work
  • Monitoring: Measuring movement or settlement
Service 2 of 6

Topographic Surveys

Precise topographic surveys capturing natural and built features including elevations, contours, vegetation, and structures.

Using GNSS, total stations, drones, and LiDAR, we deliver high-resolution data and contour maps.

Our surveys provide the spatial accuracy needed for informed decisions and streamlined project workflow.

Deliverables: CAD, GIS, and PDF formats ready for integration.

Service 3 of 6

Town Planning Services

Full range of SPLUMA applications and town planning processes for property development and land use compliance.

Services include subdivisions, consolidations, rezoning, consent use, and SPLUMA exemptions.

We liaise directly with municipalities ensuring all statutory planning requirements are met.

Achieve legal land development rights efficiently with full regulatory compliance.

Service 4 of 6

Township Surveys

Comprehensive township survey services for establishment and registration with the Surveyor-General.

We work closely with town planners, developers, engineers, and municipal authorities.

Using advanced GNSS, total stations, and CAD systems, we prepare General Plans for approval.

Precision, compliance, and timely delivery for residential, commercial, or mixed-use townships.

Service 5 of 6

Land Rights

Accurate property boundary delineation and secure registration within the Surveyor-General's cadastral system.

  • Subdivision and consolidation surveys
  • Beacon relocations and boundary determinations
  • Servitude and lease area surveys
  • Sectional title and farm subdivision surveys
  • Diagram and General Plan preparation
Service 6 of 6

Mine Surveys

Precise mine surveying for surface and underground operations from exploration to closure.

  • Open-pit and underground surveys
  • Volume calculations and stockpile measurements
  • Drill and blast design support
  • Underground control network establishment
  • Compliance surveys for DMRE statutory reporting

Full compliance with Mine Health and Safety Act requirements.
